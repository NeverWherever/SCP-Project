Mission Statement

The SCP Foundation exists to Secure, Contain, and Protect humanity from anomalous objects, entities, and phenomena that defy natural law. Operating clandestinely, we work to maintain normalcy and prevent widespread panic while studying these anomalies for the betterment and survival of mankind.

What is an SCP?

SCP stands for "Special Containment Procedures" and also refers to the anomalous items, entities, or phenomena themselves. An SCP is anything that violates the known laws of nature—objects that shouldn't exist, creatures that defy biological classification, or locations where reality itself breaks down.

Each SCP is assigned a unique designation (e.g., SCP-173, SCP-682) and classified based on the difficulty of containment and potential danger to human life and society.

Object Classifications:

  • SAFE: Easily and reliably contained
  • EUCLID: Unpredictable or not fully understood
  • KETER: Extremely difficult to contain

A Brief History

The Foundation's origins trace back to ████████, when a coalition of governments and private organizations recognized the need for a unified response to anomalous phenomena. What began as a small ████████ operation has grown into a global network of facilities, personnel, and resources.

Throughout the 20th century, Foundation influence expanded across every continent. From containing reality-bending entities to preventing potential XK-Class End-of-the-World scenarios, the Foundation has operated in the shadows, ensuring that humanity remains unaware of the true nature of our reality.

Today, the Foundation employs hundreds of thousands of personnel worldwide, from researchers and containment specialists to field agents and administrative staff. Our network of ███ Sites and Areas operates continuously to maintain the veil between the normal and the anomalous.

⚠ REMINDER TO ALL PERSONNEL ⚠

Unauthorized disclosure of SCP information is grounds for immediate termination and administration of Class-A amnestics. The Foundation's work must remain secret to prevent mass hysteria and societal collapse.

We die in the dark so you can live in the light.

Organizational Structure

The Foundation operates under the direction of the O5 Council, a committee of ██ individuals with Level 5 security clearance. Below the O5 Council, operations are divided into departments including Research, Containment, Security, and more.

Each Site operates with varying degrees of autonomy, specializing in different types of anomalies. Mobile Task Forces (MTFs) serve as rapid response teams, deployed globally to contain new anomalies or respond to containment breaches.

Security Clearance Levels

Access to SCP information is strictly controlled through a tiered clearance system:

  • Level 0: Minimal clearance for non-essential personnel
  • Level 1: Access to most Safe-class SCPs
  • Level 2: Access to most Euclid-class SCPs
  • Level 3: Senior researchers and administrators
  • Level 4: Site Directors and senior command
  • Level 5: O5 Council only
